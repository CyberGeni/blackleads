    • Who we are

    We are a youth-led, non for profit organization that strives to activate leadership qualities in young persons that do not have the opportunity to equip themselves with the core values and soft skills that are required to compete in todayâ€™s global landscape

    Blackleads is a social venture that was born from the need to enhance the quality of education of young persons in senior secondary schools in Nigeria.

    We aim to train 4500 students over the next 5 years. Practical leadership workshops form the core method of empowering these young students and achieving our purpose of activating their inherent leadership qualities.

    These workshops would focus on 3 core skills that we believe would serve as a solid foundation for other leadership skills to be built upon.

    Emotional Intelligence

    Innovation and Creativity

    Digital Literacy
